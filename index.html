EV range & run time

Range is set almost entirely by consumption — which swings 20–40% with speed, terrain, temperature, climate and load. The figure below is ideal from your inputs; plan on a margin, never the ideal. We don't model your vehicle — you supply the consumption, we do the arithmetic.

Solar charging

We add no sun, weather or location data. You tell us the share of rated output your panels are actually producing — full sun, cloud or shade is your call, as a %. Everything below is arithmetic on the figures you enter. Panels and blankets are the same here: a 400 W roof panel or a 200 W blanket both just enter as watts per unit.
